About the Club

The FPGA-AI-Embedded Club is a research-focused community dedicated to promoting innovation in FPGA design, AI integration, and embedded systems. Our mission is to create a platform where students interested in research can connect, collaborate, and grow together.

Activities

  • Hands-on FPGA Development Workshops
  • Seminars on AI Technologies and Hardware Acceleration
  • Research and Project Collaboration
  • Student-led Discussion Forums and Networking

Goal

Our goal is to empower students to explore real-world applications by combining FPGA hardware and AI techniques, fostering innovative research, and helping members grow their skills in embedded system design and development through collaborative projects and technical activities.
